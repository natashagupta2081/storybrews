about storybrews


StoryBrews is a Marketing and Communications firm based on the principles of transparency and trust. Driven with these values, we help you customize the communications strategy that is just right for your business. With many years of hands-on experience into Public Relations, Media Relations, Brand Collaboration, Thought Leadership, and Leader Profile Building under our belt, we brew solutions that establish and sustain your presence in the fast-paced world of business.

Our Team

Natasha Gupta | Founder and CEO

Natasha is a seasoned communications professional with around 20 years of experience in integrated marketing communications. She has worked in industries such as Lifestyle, Internet & Startups, Real Estate, Education, Travel, Banking, Insurance and Entertainment. Natasha had the fortune of working with many iconic brands to drive their 360 communications – from strategy to execution. Her core competencies include PR, brand alliances, media relations, industry body associations, building thought leadership, Influencer marketing and public profile building for leaders. Story Brews is the Founder’s endeavor to partner with promising brands, where she can leverage her experience to build market reputation and visibility, thus mutually benefiting in the process. Natasha loves what she does. She enjoys the challenge of a new assignment, passionate about delivering results, and try to have fun along the way. Natasha has won a handful of reputed awards like Realty plus Women Achiever in Public Relations for Real Estate category, Exchange4media Emerging Entrepreneur Award 2023.
